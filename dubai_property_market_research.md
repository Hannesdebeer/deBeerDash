## 6. Supply Pipeline: New Villa/Townhouse Units

### Overall Residential Delivery Schedule

| Year | Total Units | Villas/Townhouses | Apartments |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2025 | ~44,000 - 61,800 delivered | Limited | Majority |
| 2026 | ~42,000 - 45,000 scheduled | ~15,284 | ~99,686 |
| 2027 | Higher volumes expected | ~5,631 | Surge expected |
| Through 2028 | ~366,000 total pipeline | Limited share | Dominant |

### Key Supply Findings
- **Villa supply remains severely constrained** - only 15,284 villas scheduled for 2026 vs ~99,686 apartments
- Villa/townhouse supply represents only ~13% of total 2026 pipeline
- Undersupply in premium villa communities expected to persist through 2027

### Developer Market Share (Villa/Townhouse Pipeline)
| Developer | Units | Market Share |
|---|---|---|
| DAMAC Properties | 6,018 | 34% (most aggressive) |
| Emaar | 2,744 completed + 3,468 pipeline | 20% |
| Other developers | ~46% combined | Various |

### Villa Communities with Limited New Supply (Price Support)
- Arabian Ranches
- Dubai Hills Estate
- Jumeirah Park
- Palm Jumeirah

### Key Delivery Notes
- Slowdown in handovers expected in 2025-2026
- Unprecedented surge in apartment deliveries in 2027
- Villa handovers in 2026 concentrated in Dubailand, Al Furjan, and MBR City
- Construction delays common - not all scheduled units deliver on time

## 7. Major Agency Forecasts for 2026

### Knight Frank
- **Mainstream residential:** ~1% price growth by end of 2026
- **Prime segment:** ~3% price growth
- Overall residential: 3% - 5% for full year
- Ranked Dubai #1 globally for prime residential capital value growth

### Savills
- Prime values to rise an additional 4% - 5.9%
- Over 50% of Dubai's new villas/townhouses will be built by just two developers (DAMAC + Emaar)
- Strong momentum from 2025 expected to continue

### ValuStrat
- Rental market set for stabilisation in 2026
- December 2025 benchmark: AED 1,689/sqft citywide (+19.8% YoY)
- Villa VPI at 216 points (Jan 2021 = 100)

### Consensus Forecast (Multiple Agencies)
| Segment | 2026 Forecast |
|---|---|
| Overall residential | +3% to +6% |
| Villas | +6% to +10% |
| Townhouses | +11% to +13% |
| Prime locations | Higher end of range |
| Downside scenario | -5% |
| Optimistic scenario | +8% |

### Key Themes from Analysts
1. **Moderation, not correction** - Growth will slow from 15-30% to single digits
2. **Villas outperform apartments** - Limited supply vs abundant apartment pipeline
3. **Townhouses strongest growth segment** - 11-13% projected appreciation
4. **Supply risk mainly for apartments** - ~100K apartment units in 2026 vs ~15K villas
5. **Rental growth moderating** - From 10%+ to 3-5% in established communities
6. **Population growth absorbs supply** - Key assumption underpinning bullish forecasts
